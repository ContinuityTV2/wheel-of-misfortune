Wheel of Misfortune

Role Playing Game for Incident Management Training
Inspired by the Site Reliability Engineering book

×

Game Master: Insert your incident scenarios (in JSON format) into the "general_incidents.json" file inside the incidents folder. Use "label" for the title of the incident and "description" for the description of the incident. Pick a trainee to spin the wheel and solve the incident.
You can read a comprehensvie example on how to conduct the exercise here, but, feel free to improvise on the roles, duration and frequency. Have fun!
